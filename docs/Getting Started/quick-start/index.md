### Creating an Account

Navigate to the CICDoo [sign-up]() page and complete the registration form:


<Image src="https://files.readme.io/61c3341efe66a40154d312c526a10e03c68f01cd86f888d8418179b7b8217ea2-image.png" align="center" width="50%" />


| Field    | Description                                             |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Name     | Your full name                                          |
| Email    | Used for login and notifications                        |
| Company  | If you are a company ( optional )                       |
| Password | Minimum length enforced; shown/hidden with the eye icon |

After submitting, a verification email is sent to the address you provided. Click the link inside to activate your account before logging in.

You can also register instantly using **Google OAuth** or **GitHub OAuth** — no password required when using these providers.

### Logging In


<Image src="https://files.readme.io/1274a9a35e325b1913276d32c2cc99eccf40556ba4c8e7ecfe08b7fa6b7b2da0-image.png" align="center" width="50%" />


1. Enter your **email** and **password**.
2. Optionally check **Remember me** to stay signed in across browser sessions.
3. Click **Log In**.

Single-sign-on alternatives are available at the bottom of the login form:

- **Continue with Google**
- **Continue with GitHub**

### Password Recovery

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link and follow the instructions.
4. Choose a new password on the reset page.
